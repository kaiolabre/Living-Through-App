About Us

We Care. We Listen

Every person and every community is affected by mental ill-health and suicide in some way.

At Living Through, we understand prevention approaches in order to bring people the oppportunity to make a difference in the live of others and in themselves.

Why was the project initalised?

We Like To Help The idea of creating a suicide prevention platform was initially implemented by the fact of suicide being an undoubted reality in the Australian community currently. Thus, suicide prevention training and programs are strongly encouraged by the Australian Health Department in both private and public environments.

The project is developed in the hope of bringing more meanings to life by helping young people having a basic understanding about suicide, identify the warning signs and how to start a conversation in order to prevent it or even help themselves.

SUICIDE AWARENESS IS THE KEY

Having a platform that can make a difference in the lives of people, providing some useful features to reach those in need by recognising the signs, create a safety plan and promote useful resources to stay healthy.
