1. Living Through This
We want to live!

The first thing to do is: press the pause button.

Life can get diffult sometimes and it requires us to manage many things in our lives. But WAIT, press the pause button, you don't need to take any major action now.
2. Organise Your Thoughts
Write down your needs

Thoughts are the reason for our actions. Organising your thoughts help you see life more clearly, write down everything that you are feeling and the reason why you are feeling that way, also write down the things you need to do in order to solve things out so you can live through this and overcome your problems.

Check out "My Safety Plan" to help you priotize your thoughst
3. Connect
Start A Conversation

It is essential that people have a social life, it's in our nature. Connecting with people that you trust, sharing your thoughts and starting a conversation are great ways to go when dealing with personal issues

You are able to Anonynously talk to someone online in our website: www.livingthrouhg.co
4. Professional Help
Seeing A Psychologist

You are NOT weak, and people that see a psychologist are not weak nor crazy. Everyone needs a psychologist, for their own good, to help them achieving a healthier mind.

You can search for professional in your area by accessing "Professional Help In My Area".
